Dream Travel List


Welcome! This is a list of some of the places i'd really like to travel to someday, in no particular order.

Leshan Giant Buddha

Behold, giant Buddha. Saw this on Disney's Abominable and immediately added this place to my list.

Seoul, South Korea

Korea in itself is beautiful, but I really just love the food. I'm also half korean so it'd be a chance for me to connect more with the culture.

Egypt

Ancient Egyptians contributed so much to modern society - calendars, language, math, medicine, etc. It'd be really humbling to be able to experience those ancient temples and walk through those same ruins that they did.

Peru

Honestly just looks beautiful and Peru itself is a beautiful country.

Antelope Canyon

The whole Grand Canyon is definitely on the list, but it's something about the Antelope Canyon that really draws my attention.

Thanks for looking through! Hopefully one of these places made it to your list too.
